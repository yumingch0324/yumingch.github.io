About

I am a CSE‑CEE Ph.D. student at Georgia Tech working on the intersection of transportation systems and modern foundation models. My focus is building practical, reliable end‑to‑end autonomous driving stacks by combining world models with vision‑language‑action (VLA) policies, and quantifying uncertainty for risk‑aware planning.

Research Areas

Generative AI for AVs

LLM/VLM/VLA agents for interpretable reasoning, data‑efficient behavior cloning, and scenario generation.

World Models

Lightweight, rollout‑aware prediction in latent BEV to anticipate interactions and enable proactive planning.

Risk‑Aware Planning

Conformal prediction and CVaR objectives to handle corner cases and quantify uncertainty at inference time.

Simulation & Tooling

CARLA×SUMO co‑simulation, ROS/ROS2 bridges, and evaluation suites for human‑like “slackness.”
